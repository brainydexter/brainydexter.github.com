Revenge of the Tanks

A 2D turn-based game inspired by Scorched Earth from the days of MSDOS. It is an independently developed game by me. I wanted to see if I could complete a game in ten days, and so I was able to get the main game-play done within ten days. It was a good exercise to put the experience into some good use and figure out problems that I had never come across in such a short time.
